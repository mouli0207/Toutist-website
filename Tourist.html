Favourite Places

Taj Mahal

he Taj Mahal is located on the right bank of the Yamuna River in a vast Mughal garden that encompasses nearly 17 hectares, in the Agra District in Uttar Pradesh. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal with construction starting in 1632 AD and completed in 1648 AD, with the mosque, the guest house and the main gateway on the south, the outer courtyard and its cloisters were added subsequently and completed in 1653 AD.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a revered Sikh religious site located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is the holiest shrine in Sikhism and a central pilgrimage destination for Sikhs worldwide.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace and a royal residence. It is located in Mysore, Karnataka, India. It used to be the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and the seat of the Kingdom of Mysore. The palace is in the centre of Mysore, and faces the Chamundi Hills eastward .

Varanasi Temple

Varanasi is home to numerous revered Hindu temples, with the Shri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (also known as the Golden Temple) being one of the most prominent. This temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is considered one of the twelve Jyotirlingas
